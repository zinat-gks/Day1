🏦 Central Bank Tycoon

You run the nation's central bank. Set the interest rate each quarter to keep inflation near 2% and unemployment low. Simple to learn — tricky to master.

How to play: Each quarter you set one number — the interest rate. Raise it to cool inflation (but jobs suffer). Lower it to fight unemployment (but prices heat up). The closer you keep inflation to the 2% target and unemployment low, the more points you score. Random shocks will test you. Survive your full term!
